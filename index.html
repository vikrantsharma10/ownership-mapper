HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. One clear picture.

STEP 01 /
Enter your process steps

Add each step in the table — one row per step. Name who does it, whether they sit inside your team, in another internal team or with an external vendor, and how long it typically takes. If steps run at the same time, note that too. Write it the way you'd explain it to a new joiner.

→
STEP 02 /
AI splits the ownership

The engine classifies every step as Direct Internal, Cross-functional Internal, External Vendor or Shared — identifies your critical path, flags the hidden queue time nobody is tracking and maps which delays are yours to fix versus yours to negotiate.

→
STEP 03 /
Act on the split

Two outputs — a 150-word executive summary with your SLA gap and top two fixes, and a full team-level diagnosis with every step mapped, every lever named and fixes sequenced by critical path impact.

WHAT YOU GET

Two layers. One truth.

Layer 01 — Executive ~150 WORDS

The 150-word punch.

  • Top two fixes with effort rating
  • One-line direction for internal vs external teams
Layer 02 — Team Diagnosis FOR YOUR TEAM

The full picture, for the people doing the work.

A structured breakdown your process owner or ops lead can action immediately — without a consultant, without a workshop.

  • Full ownership map — every step tagged Direct Internal / Cross-functional / External Vendor / Shared
  • Who owns the delay — Yours to Fix / Yours to Influence / Yours to Negotiate
  • The time nobody is tracking — queue gap flagged per external step
  • Fixes sequenced by critical path impact — effort rating and dependency flag built in
  • Stacked impact table — conservative SLA reduction per fix
