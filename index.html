HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. One clear picture.

STEP 01 /
Enter your process steps

Add each step in the table — one row per step. Name who does it, the ownership type, how long it typically takes in practice and how long the actual work takes when someone sits down and does it. Note whether the handoff to the next step is automated or manual.

→
STEP 02 /
AI splits the ownership

The engine classifies every step by ownership type, calculates the gap between actual task time and real elapsed time, identifies your critical path and flags where the delay is yours to fix internally versus yours to negotiate externally.

→
STEP 03 /
Act on the split

Two outputs sequenced by your role — a reality check on whether your SLA target is even achievable given your external dependencies, a full gap analysis ranked by where the most time is recoverable and five diagnostic questions per bottleneck step to walk into your next conversation with.

WHAT YOU GET

Two layers. One truth.

Executive Leadership Diagnosis FOR VPs AND DIRECTORS

The reality check.

A sharp, board-ready output your VP can drop directly into a QBR or quarterly review. Sequenced for who needs to read it first.

  • Gap analysis table ranked by recoverable time per step
  • Category of problem named per top bottleneck; design, capacity or scheduling
  • Five diagnostic questions per top two steps as conversation starters
  • Honest ceiling — what's achievable internally versus what requires negotiation
Process Owner Diagnosis FOR YOUR TEAM

The full gap breakdown, for the people doing the work.

A structured breakdown your process owner or ops lead can action immediately — without a consultant, without a workshop.
